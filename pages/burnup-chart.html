A burnup chart displays developer progress in completing ("burning") story points against the total story points for the current release.
The primary purpose of the chart is to show at a glance whether the hoped-for release is feasible, or will have to be rescoped. Secondarily, the chart shows how developer velocity is trending, and how project scope has been changing.
For a simple spreadsheet that generates a burnup chart, feel free to make a copy of this template.
