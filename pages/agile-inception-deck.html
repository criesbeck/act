The agile inception deck is a handful of slides to prepare in advance and share with your developers. Less is more. A few slides, not a hundred pages of requirements. The goal is to enlighten, no overwhelm.
Exactly what slides to use are up to you, but my recommendations are to include the following:
  • a 4-panel storyboard
  • a product box
  • a use case diagram or similar stakeholders slide
  • a "what keeps us up at night"
The original description of an agile inception deck appeared on the Agile Samurai website. I recommend deferring scope discussions until later, and deferring architecture decisions to the developers.
