Scenario: a story about a product providing a payoff to a person with a problem.
Person: someone with a name who typifies your target user.
Payoff: the delivery of specific value to that person.
A scenario is one of the most important tools in the early stages of product development. A scenario articulates the value of your product and the target market. A scenario is the basis for your first demos. A scenario determines the scope and priority of user stories in the backlog.
Use scenarios to define your product. See Kim Goodwin's outstanding Story-Telling by Design.
Use a 4-panel storyboard to develop and present your scenario in a compact visual form.
Use a scenario canvas to organize your user stories by the scenarios they enable.
