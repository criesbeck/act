The Kano model separates product functionality into delighters, performance items and must-haves. Customers won't buy your product unless it includes the must-haves, and meets expected performance standards.
This simple but intuitive model turns out to be very useful when deciding what to build in your first slices. Start with the delighter. If you don't have a good one, no one will care about the must-haves.
For more on the Kano model from an agile development context, see The Complete Guide to the Kano Model .
