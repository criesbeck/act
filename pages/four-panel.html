In at most 4 panels tell a specific story about your app. Two panels for context, persona, and problem, two panels for payoff and wrapup.
Panel 3 is the most important. Start there. Show the screenshot that demonstrates your application at its best. Then work backwards to set up a convincing story. Iterate. Test it on people.
The 4-panel is the first test of your idea and whether you are ready to start. Can you even come up with a realistic example from start to finish? Do your developers think they can build it? Are your potential customers excited by the story?
Details are critical. They make a story work. "Mary, a freshman in Calculus 102, nervous about the midterm in 3 days" not "A student needs a tutor.

Skip the friction. Don't get into logging in, menu selection, or a list of data entry steps.
