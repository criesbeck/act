A text-based UI flow sketch is a quick easy way to tell your developers how you think a sequence of user interactions should go, without getting into screen design.
Such minimal sketches are easy to explore. They can help you and your developers quickly find inconsistencies, dead ends, missing user feedback, common actions with too many steps, and so on.
Avoid detailed storyboards in early development. Those become important when an app has matured past the basic functionality stage.
